About Student Resource Hub

A web platform for XYZ’s engineering students where students, teachers and alumni can share and access academic resources in one central platform. Easily upload papers, projects, resources, or find study resources.

Faculty

Faculty can upload past papers, approve student submissions and share announcements to enhance student learning.

Student

Students can find resources, question papers, project files, industrial notes, research papers, and faculty’s work to help them in their learning process – all in one place.

Alumni

An opportunity to contribute past resources, mentor students, and share career guidance, strengthening the alumni-student connection.
